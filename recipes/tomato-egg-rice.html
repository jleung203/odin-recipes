Tomato Egg Rice


Description

Try this stir fried dish mixing the tangy tomatoes with a fluffy and savory blanket of scrambled eggs, served with classic white rice on the side. Great for a quick, comfort meal!


Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized tomatoes
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 green onions
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 cup cooked white rice

Steps

  1. Cut the tomatoes into small wedges and chop the green onions into small pieces.
  2. In a bowl, crack 4 eggs and add salt, white pepper, and sesame oil. Whisk until thoroughly mixed.
  3. Preheat the pan over medium heat until some smoke comes out. Add in the egg mixture and 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil. Stir and fold the eggs until they form a curd-like texture, and remove from the pan immediately.
  4. Turn up the heat to high and add the tomatoes, green onions, and 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil to the pan. Stir-fry for 1 minute, and then add in the sugar and ketchup.
  5. Mix together and cover the pan with a lid. Let everything cook together for 1-2 minutes until the tomatoes are softened.
  6. Uncover the pan and stir lightly for 15 to 30 seconds. Enjoy with some cooked white rice on the side!
